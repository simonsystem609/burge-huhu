Card face photos in this folder come from two sources.

1) Number cards (VII/VIII/IX/X) and court cards (Also/Felso/Kiraly), all
   4 suits — cropped from:

     "Original-Hungarian-Tell-set.jpg"
     Photo by SZERVÁC Attila, via Wikimedia Commons
     https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Original-Hungarian-Tell-set.jpg
     License: CC BY-SA 4.0 — https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/

   Modifications made: cropped into individual card images; the printed
   manufacturer mark ("PIATNIK WIEN www.piatnik.com") in the blank center
   gap of the tok-VII card was painted out with a sampled background fill.
   These derived images are themselves licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0, per the
   ShareAlike term of the source license.

2) Aces (Asz), all 4 suits — cropped from:

     "Piatnik ászok.jpg"
     Original engraving by Josef Sürch and Josef Neumayer, 1860
     https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Piatnik_%C3%A1szok.jpg
     License: Public domain (author's life + 70 years has long since passed)

Both depict the same Hungarian "Tell pattern" (magyar kártya) deck,
originally designed by József Schneider (Pest, c. 1836) and produced
largely unchanged by card manufacturers since.
